## Ocean Coral Spring Resort

### Overview
- 5-star all-inclusive resort in Trelawny
- 30 minutes from Montego Bay airport (MBJ)
- 513 rooms (junior suites and master suites)
- Recently recovered from Hurricane Melissa (Oct 2025) - fully operational

### Dining (8 Restaurants + Bars)

| Restaurant | Cuisine |
|------------|---------|
| **Buffet** | International, show cooking (Breakfast 7-10:30am, Lunch 12:30-4pm, Dinner 6:30-10pm) |
| **La Locanda** | Italian |
| **Sakura** | Teppanyaki Japanese |
| **Sugar Cane** | Jamaican/Caribbean |
| **Thali** | Indian |
| **Route 66** | American BBQ |
| **Beach Restaurant** | Beachfront Caribbean |
| **Privilege Restaurant** | Exclusive (Privilege guests only) |

**Tip:** Red Snapper beach grill is quieter at lunch than main buffet.

### Amenities
- Large pools + lazy river + water slide
- Despacio Spa Centre
- Kids' club and family activities
- Bowling, kayaking, windsurfing, volleyball
- 24-hour gym with yoga classes
- Private beach

### Dress Code
- No swimwear/flip-flops in restaurants
- Men need sleeved shirts for dinner

### Reviews (2025)
- Positive: Food variety, friendly staff, clean rooms, quick hurricane recovery
- Notes: Occasional Wi-Fi issues, bedding comfort varies by room

## Practical Tips

### Money
- Currency: Jamaican Dollar (JMD), but USD widely accepted
- Credit cards accepted at resort
- Tip in USD ($1-2 per service is appreciated)

### Packing
- Light, breathable clothing
- Reef-safe sunscreen
- Mosquito repellent
- Sleeved shirts for restaurants
- Water shoes for waterfall trips

### Airport Transfer
- MBJ airport to resort: ~30 minutes
- Resort may offer shuttle - confirm with booking
